Albert Einstein: A Tribute

Albert Einstein was born on March 14, 1879, in Ulm, Germany. From a young age, he showed an extraordinary curiosity and a deep love for mathematics and physics.

Einstein attended the Swiss Federal Polytechnic in Zurich, where he graduated in 1900. Initially struggling to find an academic position, he worked at the Swiss Patent Office, which gave him ample time to develop his theories.
